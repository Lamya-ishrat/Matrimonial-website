Model and actress Bidya Sinha Mim has not been available on the phone for several days. He is not replying to the SMS sent in the messenger. Mim then where? Mim's wedding ceremony was held at a five star hotel in Dhaka. In the afternoon, the stills of Mim's wedding went viral on Facebook.

The colorful wedding party was held near the swimming pool of a five-star hotel on Airport Road on Tuesday afternoon. Guests can be seen in white and gold in the green garden. Mim was sitting in the bride's seat on a well-arranged stage on one side of the swimming pool. Next to it is groom Soni Poddar. Before that, they are tied to the seven rules.

What happened? Everyone is now accustomed to watching the marriages of world celebrities for the benefit of social media. There must be curiosity about the country's star. Mim was no exception.
